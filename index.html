Masala Pasta

MANDATORY INGREDIENTS:
1. 1 cup penne pasta or any pasta
2. water to boil
3. 1/2 tsp salt

OTHER INGREDIENTS:
 1. 1 tsp oil
2. 1 tsp butter
3. 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
4. 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
5. 1/2 onion, finely chopped
6. 1 cup tomato pulp
7. 1/4 tsp turmeric / haldi
8. 1/2 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
9. 2 tbsp tomato sauce
10. 1/2 tsp garam masala
11. 1/2 tsp salt
12. 2 tbsp corn
13. 1/4 capsicum, finely chopped
14. 1/4 carrot, finely chopped
15. 2 tbsp peas / matar
16. 7 florets broccoli
17. 3 tbsp water
18. 1/2 tsp mixed herbs, basil, marjoram, oregano, rosemary, sage, thyme

INSTRUCTIONS
1.Firstly, saute 1 inch ginger and 2 clove garlic in 1 tsp oil and 1 tsp butter.
2.Further saute 1/2 onion till they turn soft.
3.Additionally add 1 cup tomato pulp and saute well.
4.Keep stirring till the tomato pulp thickens.
5.Now add 1/4 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp chilli powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala and 1/2 tsp salt.
6.Saute till the spices gets cooked completely.
7.Now add 2 tbsp corn, 1/4 capsicum, 1/4 carrot, 2 tbsp peas and 7 florets broccoli. Saute for a minute.
8.Add in 3 tbsp water and mix well.
9.Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
10.Now add in 2 tbsp tomato sauce and 1/2 tsp mixed herbs. mix well.
11.Add in cooked pasta and mix gently till the sauce gets coated well.
12.Finally, serve masala pasta indian style hot topped with cheese if required.
